About Me

I'm a high school student passionate about leadership, computer science, and creating opportunities for others. As the Founder and President of my school's Computer Science Club, I've built a community where students discover their passion for tech and grow together.

Through SkillsUSA, where I serve as State Treasurer, I've seen firsthand how mentorship and opportunity transform students' lives. I'm committed to ensuring that every student feels heard, supported, and empowered to reach their potential.

Leadership Building communities where students thrive and feel valued through genuine connection and clear vision.
Initiative Taking action to create opportunities, not waiting for permission—from founding a CS Club to innovations in SkillsUSA.
Impact Focused on tangible outcomes that matter: better programs, stronger communication, and real opportunities for growth.
Service Dedicated to hearing all voices and making sure our organization works for every student, not just a few.

My mission: Build a culture of leadership, opportunity, and belonging where every student knows they matter and can make an impact.
